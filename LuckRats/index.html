FIGHT AGAINST THE MONSTERS INVADING THE PLANET

The mutant moles came up from underground wanting to invade the ground - where humans live. Let's transform into monster hunters and get rewards for the winners

Immerse yourself in battle

Only through battle and win can you survive. Alone, against the monsters, even bet everything to fighting other monster hunters to win the battle and plunder their fortune.
"Win eat it all, fall back to nothing"

  • PVE: Single Player

    Battle monsters and build the experience and skills for combat with other hunters in the Arena Battle Mode.

  • PVP: Arena Battle Mode

    Team up, bet to battle other players, enter tournaments and special events and win the most valuable rewards in the game. PVP Arena is where monster hunters become true Legendary!

Compete in seasonal challenges

Moles breed very strongly and evolve diversely. After each defeat, they reappear with a more advanced evolution. This is also an opportunity for monster hunters to participate in the killing and win rewards

Every time a mutant mole species is completely destroyed, it is time to prepare for a new hunting season. Very quickly followed by the attack of mutant moles whose evolutionary with a higher level, monster hunters have a chance to train and win bigger rewards from hunting them. However, the war will also become more and more brutal and bloody!
