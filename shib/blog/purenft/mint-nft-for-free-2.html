In May 2021, FOIL have scheduled to create the first Pure NFT Marketplace with NFT crypto assets that can’t be deleted, replaced, lost, or censored unlike those NFTs that are listed on rarible, but actually just hash links to some unknown storage locations . At the same time, pure nft standard protects the blockchain community from the creation of prohibited content, because of the multilayer statuses for each ecosystem participant.

The FOIL network are happy to invite creative NFT art and digital content creators.

If you are involved in digital art, nft art, media content creation, crypto art such as:
Digital artist, illustrator, video effects maker, animation or any type of digital art.

An artist painting on canvas, asphalt, rooftop, or other art maker, involved in street art.

Creator of Gifs, Memes, and other cool stuff from the same category

Writer of articles, information content and other content related activity

Writer of poetry, prose or quotes

Sound architect, music producer, beatmaker, composer or a musician

Don’t waste your time waiting, go and register to access our Test NET!

Once you are registered in nft marketplace, our NFT minting staff at Foil Network will teach and explain to you:
The basics of our cross-platform blockchain technology, how to work with it, how to secure your wallets.

Show you how to create ideal NFT and give ideas how to realize the full potential NFT assets

Help you to connect the NFT resale commission and other functions of our NFT platform

Pay the transaction fees for the NFT storage and creation! Transaction fees of the first NFT artwork for 200 NFT crypto art creators will be paid by us.

What type of content exactly can we create as NFTs for free?

As an author you can mint NFT packed with your genuine content or artwork in just a few easy steps!

For example, your NFT token could be:

A piece of digital or physical art representing your feelings or thoughts

NFT can be cool, but intimidating

NFT can be disgusting and admirable

NFT can also be political or support any political or freedom movement

NFT can be created to support charities, donations and help someone for real!

OUR ADVICE: YOUR CRYPTO NFT CONTENT SHOULD BE GENUINE AND LEGIT. DO NOT CREATE ANY NFT ART CONTENT COMPLETED WITH INTERNATIONAL PROHIBITS, PORNO AND OTHER SIMILAR CONTENT.
